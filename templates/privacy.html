Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Spendly is a personal expense tracker built around a simple idea: your financial data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we need it, how we store it, and what choices you have. Please read it carefully.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect the basic information you provide — your name, email address, and a password (which is stored in hashed form and never stored or transmitted in plain text). When you use Spendly, we store the expense entries you choose to record: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you add yourself.

We also collect limited technical information needed to keep the service running — such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamps of sign-ins — so we can detect suspicious activity and troubleshoot problems.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide solely to operate Spendly for you. This means displaying your expense records, computing category and time-period summaries, remembering your preferences across sessions, and keeping your account secure. We do not build advertising profiles, and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information to anyone.

If you write to us for support, we will use your contact details only to respond to your request. We will not add you to a marketing list without your explicit permission.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in secure databases accessible only to authorized systems. Passwords are hashed using modern algorithms and are never visible to anyone — including us — in their original form. We use industry-standard transport encryption (HTTPS) between your browser and Spendly so that your information is protected in transit.

While no system can guarantee perfect security, we work continuously to keep your records safe. You can update or delete your expense entries at any time from within the app, and you may request deletion of your entire account and associated data by contacting us at the address below. We will complete such requests within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required to retain certain records for legal or security reasons.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of trusted third-party providers to operate — for example, a hosting platform to run the service and an email provider to send account-related messages such as sign-in links or password resets. These providers receive only the information they need to perform their role, and they are bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

We do not embed third-party advertising, tracking pixels, or social media widgets in Spendly. We do not share your expense data with any third party for marketing or analytics purposes.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the service evolves, as legal requirements change, or as we introduce new features. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the service before the changes take effect. Your continued use of Spendly after the updated policy is in effect constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app. We take every privacy request seriously and will respond as quickly as we can.
